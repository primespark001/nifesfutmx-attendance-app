Who we are

NIFES FUT Minna is the campus fellowship of the Nigeria Fellowship of Evangelical Students at Federal University of Technology Minna. We pursue campus transformation by rooting student life in Scripture, prayer and discipleship.

Mission & Vision

Mission

NIFES exists to reach out to students through evangelism and discipleship training, to mobilize them upon graduation to impact the campus, church and society with godly values.

Vision

To be a movement of Christ-like students in Nigeria's tertiary institutions transforming the campus, and society upon graduation.

Core Values

  1. Evangelical Heritage.
  2. Biblical Discipleship.
  3. Leadership Development.
  4. Organizational Integrity and Accountability.
  5. Students Initiatives.
  6. Inter-denominational and Non-denominational.
  7. Missions.
  8. Academic and Vocational Excellence.
  9. Promotion of Reading Culture particularly of the Christian Literature.
  10. Promoting Christian Maturity through small groups.
